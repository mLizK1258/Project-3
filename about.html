About me

Who am I?

As a UX designer with a strong background in web development, I am driven by my passion for creating impactful and user-friendly products that solve real-world problems. Drawing on my experience in customer service and web development, I have honed my skills in UX/UI design to create products that benefit both users and businesses. Working collaboratively with a team is essential to me, and I thrive in an environment where we can bounce ideas off one another and produce products that exceed expectations. From ideation to development, I love being involved in the entire process and seeing a project come to life.

What do I value?

I value creativity and believe thinking outside the box makes life more colorful and lively. Humility is also important to me, as there is always something to learn and improve upon. Self-reflection helps me make better decisions and learn from my mistakes, while self-discipline allows me to step out of my comfort zone and achieve my goals.

Let's connect

If you would like to chat about UX or any projects I'm working on, please feel free to contact me at giabaochu1280@gmail.com. I would love to connect with you!
